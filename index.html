LOCAL PRESS INTRODUCES NEW DIGITAL NEWS SHEET

Beautiful vintage typography adapts dynamic web links into physical print broadsheet.

By STAFF CORRESPONDENT | Source: Local Press Office

Welcome to The Press, an application designed to revive the elegance of traditional journalism in the digital age. By translating modern web content into a classic broadsheet style, it provides a focused, tactile reading experience. Every detail, from the layout grids to the typography, has been crafted to reflect the aesthetics of 19th and 20th-century newsprint.

To begin formatting your own articles, simply return to the landing page, enter any URL, and watch the printing press work. If a website is behind a paywall or has strict scrapers, you can choose the "Write From Scratch" option to copy and paste your article text directly. You can edit the text blocks on this page by clicking and typing on them.

Once you are satisfied with the styling, theme, and layout, use the "Print / PDF" button to print it out on physical paper or save it as a PDF document. Enjoy the nostalgia of reading the morning paper in a digital-first format.
